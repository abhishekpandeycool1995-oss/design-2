About Our Practice

A Whole-Person Approach to Mental Health

At Mental Wellness Montclair, we believe that healing happens when you feel truly seen and supported. Our practice was founded on the principle that effective therapy goes beyond diagnosing symptoms — it's about understanding the whole person within the context of their relationships, environment, and life experiences.

Our team of licensed therapists and evaluators provides a full spectrum of mental health services for children, adolescents, adults, and couples. We specialize in ADHD evaluations, anxiety treatment, and child and teen therapy, all delivered with warmth, empathy, and clinical excellence.

We are committed to creating a safe, affirming space where every client feels welcome regardless of background, identity, or orientation.

How It Works

Your Journey to Wellness

We make starting therapy simple and stress-free. Here is what to expect.

1

15-Min Consult

A free, no-pressure phone call to discuss your needs, answer your questions, and find the right therapist for you.

2

Intake & Goals

Your first session focuses on understanding your history, identifying goals, and creating a personalized treatment plan.

3

Therapy Sessions

Regular sessions where we work together using evidence-based approaches to help you grow, heal, and thrive.

4

Continuous Care

We regularly check in on your progress and adjust your treatment plan to ensure you are getting the most out of therapy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our practice and therapy process.

Your first session is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. We will discuss what brought you to therapy, your personal history, and what you hope to achieve. Together, we will develop a treatment plan tailored to your needs. There is no pressure — you can go at your own pace.
