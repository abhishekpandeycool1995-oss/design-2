Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about therapy at Mental Wellness Montclair. If you do not find your answer here, please reach out.

Your first session, often called an intake session, is about getting to know each other. Your therapist will ask about what brought you to therapy, your personal history, current challenges, and what you hope to achieve. There is no pressure to share more than you are comfortable with. Together, you will begin to develop a treatment plan tailored to your needs. Sessions typically last 50 minutes.

Booking is easy. You can call us at (973) 210-7310, email admin@mentalwellnessmontclair.com, or fill out the contact form on our website. We will respond within 24 hours to schedule a free 15-minute phone consultation. During this call, we will discuss your needs, answer your questions, and match you with the therapist best suited to help you.
Yes, we offer secure telehealth sessions for clients located anywhere in New Jersey. Virtual therapy is conducted through a HIPAA-compliant video platform that works on your computer, tablet, or smartphone. Research shows that online therapy is just as effective as in-person therapy for most conditions. You can choose in-person, virtual, or a mix of both.
The duration of therapy varies based on your individual needs and goals. Some clients find that 8-12 sessions are sufficient to address a specific concern, while others benefit from longer-term therapy for deeper exploration or ongoing support. We will discuss a timeline during your intake and regularly check in on your progress to adjust as needed.
We provide services for children (ages 4+), adolescents (ages 13-19), adults, and couples. Our clinicians have specialized training in working with each age group, and we carefully match clients with the therapist whose expertise aligns with their needs.
Therapists (psychologists, social workers, counselors) provide talk therapy to help you work through emotional, behavioral, and relationship challenges. Psychiatrists are medical doctors who can prescribe medication and often focus on the biological aspects of mental health. At Mental Wellness Montclair, we provide therapy and evaluations. If medication may be helpful, we collaborate with psychiatrists in the community and can provide a referral.
We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before your appointment, or missed sessions, are charged the full session fee. We understand that emergencies happen, and we handle these on a case-by-case basis.
Yes, therapy is confidential. Everything you share with your therapist is protected by law and ethical guidelines. There are a few exceptions: if there is a risk of harm to yourself or someone else, if we suspect abuse or neglect of a child or vulnerable adult, or if a court orders release of records. We will always discuss these limits with you during your first session.
We understand that finding the right therapist is important. During your free 15-minute consultation, we will discuss your needs, preferences, and goals. Our intake coordinator will recommend a therapist whose expertise, style, and availability align with what you are looking for. If at any point you feel the fit is not right, we are happy to help you transition to another clinician within our practice.
