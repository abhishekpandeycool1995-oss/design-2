Our Services

Comprehensive mental health care for every stage of life — from childhood through adulthood, in-person and online.

Evaluations

ADD/ADHD Evaluations

Our comprehensive ADHD evaluation process is designed to provide clarity and direction. We understand that ADHD presents differently in everyone, and a thorough assessment is essential for an accurate diagnosis and effective treatment plan.

Our evaluations include a detailed clinical interview, standardized rating scales (completed by clients, parents, and teachers when applicable), cognitive screening, and a thorough review of developmental, academic, and family history. We also screen for co-occurring conditions such as anxiety, depression, and learning disabilities.

At the conclusion of the evaluation, you will receive a comprehensive written report with diagnostic findings, personalized recommendations, and practical strategies for home, school, or work.

  • Comprehensive clinical interviews
  • Standardized rating scales & cognitive screening
  • Detailed written report with recommendations
  • School and workplace accommodation support
Children 4-12

Child Therapy

Children experience big feelings, and they do not always have the words to express them. Our child therapy services use play-based techniques, expressive arts, and age-appropriate talk therapy to help children ages 4 through 12 navigate their emotional world.

We work with children experiencing anxiety, ADHD, behavioral challenges, social difficulties, grief, family transitions (divorce, relocation), and trauma. Parent involvement is a key component of our approach — we provide regular feedback sessions and practical strategies to support your child at home.

  • Play therapy & expressive arts interventions
  • Emotion regulation & coping skill building
  • Parent coaching & family integration
  • School collaboration & advocacy
Adolescents 13-19

Teen Therapy

Adolescence is a time of tremendous change, growth, and sometimes struggle. Our teen therapy services provide a confidential, nonjudgmental space where adolescents can explore their identities, process their feelings, and build the skills they need to navigate this complex stage of life.

We help teens with anxiety, depression, academic stress, peer relationships, social media pressures, identity exploration, and family conflict. Our therapists are skilled at meeting teens where they are, using a blend of talk therapy, mindfulness, and skill-building approaches.

  • Anxiety, depression & stress management
  • Identity exploration & self-esteem building
  • Academic & peer relationship support
  • Family communication coaching
Anxiety & Mood

Anxiety Therapy

Anxiety is one of the most common reasons people seek therapy — and it is also one of the most treatable. Our anxiety therapy services are grounded in evidence-based approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), exposure therapy, and mindfulness-based stress reduction.

We treat generalized anxiety disorder, social anxiety, panic disorder, specific phobias, and health anxiety. Our goal is not to eliminate anxiety entirely (which is neither realistic nor helpful) but to help you develop a healthier relationship with it — so that it no longer controls your choices or limits your life.

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Exposure & response prevention (ERP)
  • Mindfulness & relaxation techniques
  • Relapse prevention & long-term resilience
Relationships

Couples Therapy

Relationships take work, and sometimes every couple needs a little extra support. Our couples therapy services help partners strengthen their connection, improve communication, and work through challenges in a safe, neutral environment.

Whether you are facing a specific crisis, navigating a life transition, or simply want to deepen your connection, our therapists provide a structured, supportive space to help you and your partner grow together. We use Gottman Method principles, Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), and other evidence-based approaches.

  • Communication & conflict resolution skills
  • Trust rebuilding & intimacy enhancement
  • Premarital counseling & life transition support
  • Gottman Method & EFT based approaches
Telehealth

Virtual Therapy

Life is busy, and getting to an office is not always possible. Our virtual therapy services bring the same compassionate, evidence-based care directly to you through a secure, HIPAA-compliant telehealth platform.

Virtual therapy is available to anyone located in New Jersey. Sessions are conducted via live video and are just as effective as in-person therapy for most concerns. Whether you are at home, at work, or traveling, you can access the support you need on your schedule.

  • HIPAA-compliant secure video platform
  • Same therapists, same quality of care
  • Flexible scheduling including evenings & weekends
  • Available for individuals, teens, and couples
