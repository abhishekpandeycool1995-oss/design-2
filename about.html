About Our Practice

For over a decade, we have been providing compassionate, whole-person mental health care to the Montclair community and beyond.

Our Philosophy

Healing Happens in Relationship

At Mental Wellness Montclair, we believe that the foundation of effective therapy is a strong, trusting relationship between client and therapist. We approach every person with warmth, respect, and genuine curiosity about their unique story.

Our philosophy is rooted in a whole-person, integrative approach. We do not just treat symptoms — we consider the full spectrum of your life: your relationships, your environment, your values, and your goals. This allows us to create treatment plans that are deeply personalized and truly effective.

We are committed to providing culturally responsive, affirming care to clients of all backgrounds, identities, and orientations. Our team regularly engages in ongoing education to ensure our approaches remain current, inclusive, and evidence-based.

Our Story

A Practice Built on Connection

Mental Wellness Montclair was founded in response to a growing need for accessible, high-quality mental health care in Essex County. Our founder recognized that many people struggled to find therapists who truly listened, who offered flexible scheduling, and who provided a full range of services under one roof.

Starting with a single therapist and a small office, we have grown into a multi-clinician practice offering comprehensive evaluations, individual therapy, family therapy, and couples counseling. What has not changed is our commitment to putting the client at the center of everything we do.

Today, our team includes licensed psychologists, clinical social workers, and professional counselors, each bringing their own expertise and therapeutic style. Together, we serve hundreds of clients from Montclair and throughout New Jersey.

Our Approach

Evidence-Based, Personalized Care

We integrate multiple therapeutic modalities to create a treatment plan that works best for each individual.

CBT

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy helps you identify and change unhelpful thought patterns and behaviors.

Mindfulness-Based

Integrating mindfulness practices to increase present-moment awareness and reduce stress.

Psychodynamic

Exploring how past experiences and unconscious patterns influence your present thoughts and behaviors.

Family Systems

Understanding individuals within the context of their family relationships and dynamics.
