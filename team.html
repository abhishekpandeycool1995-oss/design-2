Meet Our Team

Clinical Team

Your Therapists

Each member of our clinical team is licensed, experienced, and dedicated to providing the highest quality of care.

Photo
Clinical Psychologist & Director

Dr. Sarah Mitchell

Ph.D., Licensed Clinical Psychologist

Dr. Mitchell is the founder and clinical director of Mental Wellness Montclair. She earned her Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from Rutgers University and has over 12 years of experience in private practice and community mental health settings.

She specializes in comprehensive ADHD evaluations for children and adults, anxiety disorders, and child and adolescent therapy. Dr. Mitchell takes an integrative approach, drawing primarily from CBT, psychodynamic theory, and mindfulness-based practices. She is passionate about destigmatizing mental health and making quality care accessible to all.

Dr. Mitchell provides clinical supervision to the team and is actively involved in training the next generation of therapists.

Photo
Licensed Clinical Social Worker

James Rivera, LCSW

MSW, Licensed Clinical Social Worker

James earned his Master of Social Work from Columbia University and has been practicing for over 8 years. He specializes in couples therapy, anxiety treatment, and trauma-informed care for adults and adolescents.

James is trained in Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) for couples, CBT for anxiety and depression, and EMDR for trauma processing. His style is warm, direct, and collaborative. He believes that therapy should be a space where you feel both supported and challenged to grow.

In addition to his clinical work, James facilitates a monthly men’s support group and provides clinical supervision to graduate social work interns.

Photo
Licensed Professional Counselor

Emily Chen, LPC

MA, Licensed Professional Counselor

Emily holds a Master’s in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Seton Hall University and has 6 years of experience working with children, adolescents, and families. She specializes in play therapy, child anxiety, and behavioral challenges.

Emily is a Registered Play Therapist (RPT) and uses play-based interventions, expressive arts, and sand tray therapy to help young clients express themselves and work through difficult emotions. She partners closely with parents and schools to create a consistent support system around each child.

Emily is also trained in Trauma-Focused CBT and provides parent coaching sessions to help caregivers reinforce therapeutic skills at home.

Photo
Licensed Marriage & Family Therapist

Dr. Amara Okafor

Ph.D., LMFT

Dr. Okafor earned her Ph.D. in Marriage and Family Therapy from Drexel University and brings 10 years of experience working with couples, families, and individuals. She specializes in relationship issues, life transitions, and identity exploration.

Dr. Okafor is trained in the Gottman Method, Emotionally Focused Therapy, and Narrative Therapy. She has a particular interest in working with multicultural and multiracial couples and individuals navigating questions of identity and belonging. She creates a warm, affirming space for clients to explore all facets of who they are.

Dr. Okafor is also a published researcher and presents regularly at national conferences on culturally responsive therapy practices.
